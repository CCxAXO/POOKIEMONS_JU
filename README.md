
# CarbonCoin
by Team POOKIEMONS




## Features

-Transparent tracking of carbon emissions using blockchain technology

-Automated verification through smart contracts

-Real-time data integration via IoT sensors and APIs from trusted sources.

-Decentralized marketplace to trade verified carbon credits securely.

-Every emission recorded, verified, and tokenized, creating a digital proof of sustainability.
## Impact and Benefits

- Verified Environmental Accountability: Ensures transparent, tamper-proof carbon accounting, discouraging greenwashing and driving responsible energy use.

- Stronger Organizational Sustainability: Provides clear emissions data that boost investor confidence, streamline compliance, and enable creation of tradable carbon credits.

- Empowered Individuals: Allows users to track and offset personal carbon footprints, supported by gamified tools that encourage sustainable behavior.

- Green Economy Enablement: Accelerates the growth of digital carbon markets and fosters innovation in clean-tech analytics, creating opportunities for startups, governments, and NGOs.

- Emission-Linked Economic Value: Introduces a dynamic model where market value rises or falls based on actual carbon emission performance.
